About the Project

Bias in the Modern World is our broader look at how the assumptions and generalizations people make can impact other peoples lives. We will look at data from a variety of sources and disseminate that information to the public in easy to grasp pieces that will easily fit into debates and discussion with family or peers. We will research hiring bias in STEM fields, how bias changes the outcomes of custody battles and divorce cases, and many more instances of important but often overlooked trends of discrimination from many sides.
